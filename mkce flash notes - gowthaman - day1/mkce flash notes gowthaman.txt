MOVING BALL:
============
STEP 1: DRAW OVAL and remove outline
STEP 2: select 35th frame and right click (insert key frame)
step 3: ball move to right side corner
step 4: right click inbetween frame --> create motion tween
step 5: run-->enter (one time same window)
        run --> ctrl+enter (continous animation o/p window)
