GUIDE Layer:
============
	step 1: layer1 -->  draw oval without outline

	step 2: layer1 --> right click --> add motion guide

	step 3: in Guide Layer --> 50th frame -->f5 or (right click -->insert frame)

	step 4: layer1 --> 50th frame --> insert key frame (f6)

	step 5: in guide layer -->1st frame click--> 
               draw one curve using pen or pencil tool
	       -->click first frame in guide layer.	

	step 6: selection tool click
	
	step 7: drag the oval and place start point of curve.
	step 8 --> click 50th frame in layer or guide layer.
	step 9: drag the oval and place end point of curve.
	step 10: in between layer1 -->right click -->create motion tween
	step 11: run
