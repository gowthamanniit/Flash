Guide Layer:
============
step 1:draw circle(layer1)
step 2:right click on layer1 -->click add motion guide
       -->draw curve using pen or pencil 
step 3: right click on guide layer's 50th frame -->insert frame(f5)
step 4: right click on layer1's 50th frame -->insert key frame(f6)
setp 5: click frame1 on layer1-->click selection tool-->
click&drag on circle's center position and place staring postion of curve.

setp 6: click frame50 on layer1
click&drag on circle's center position and place ending postion of curve.
step 7: in layer inbetween --> right click -->create motion tween.
step 8: run
